Solar-Powered Occupancy Sensing System

Team Members: Nayiri Krzysztofowicz, Will Define, Kathryn Wason, Bryan Rombach

The team's goal was to design and model a self-powered room occupancy monitoring system. We began by determining the amount of energy typically available in our harvesting environment, indoor study areas. We then benchmarked the required system load components to arrive at a reasonable sampling period for each component and duty cycle period for the entire load system. The resulting load power trace was simulated alongside the expected power trace from the energy harvesting analysis to arrive to conclusive results about the performance of our system.
